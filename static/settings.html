How to connect Xero — step by step
  1. Open developer.xero.com → My Apps and pick the app (currently “Automate accounting”), or create a new one (Web app).
  2. On its Configuration page: copy the Client ID, generate a Client Secret, and add the Redirect URI shown above to OAuth 2.0 redirect URIs.
  3. Paste Client ID + Client Secret here and press Save credentials. (Tenant ID and Redirect URI are prefilled — leave them unless the Xero org or domain changes.)
  4. Press Connect to Xero, log in, and approve access for the organisation. You'll land on a “Xero Connected” page.
  5. Done — the badge above turns Connected. The P&L “Sync from Xero” button and Wise reconciliation now work without pasting tokens, and a weekly keep-alive prevents the 60-day token expiry.
